Our Mission

The RAISE Vision

Responsible Artificial Intelligence for a Smart Economy (RAISE) is an initiative to position New Zealand at the forefront of shaping trustworthy and safe AI for everyone. We bring together top academics, industry experts, and Māori innovation networks to advocate for technologies that are transparent, explainable, and operate in ways people can understand.

The coalition and ambition remain stronger than ever. We continue to work towards a future where AI in Aotearoa is built and used with our highest values in mind—integrity, human rights, and transparency.

Advocating for Change

Realizing responsible AI requires more than just technical solutions; it demands robust governance and active public engagement. The RAISE team is leading the charge for AI regulation in New Zealand.

Many of our members are signatories to the open letter calling on the New Zealand Parliament to lead a bipartisan effort for risk management-based AI regulation and the establishment of a national AI oversight body.
