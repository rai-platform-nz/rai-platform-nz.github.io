The RAISE Vision

Responsible Artificial Intelligence for a Smart Economy (RAISE) envisions New Zealand at the forefront of trustworthy and safe AI. As a proposed national centre of gravity for AI research, policy, and commercialisation, RAISE would unite top academics, industry experts, and Māori innovation networks to advocate for technologies that are transparent, explainable, and operate in ways people can understand.

We aspire to build a future where AI in Aotearoa is developed with our highest values in mind—integrity, human rights, and transparency.

Why New Zealand? Our scale and social cohesion make Aotearoa the ideal place to study the real-world societal impacts of AI deployment. RAISE positions NZ as a world-leading centre for sociotechnical evaluation, translating research into enduring benefits for our economy and society.

Advocating for Change

Realizing responsible AI requires more than just technical solutions; it demands robust governance and active public engagement. RAISE aims to serve as a trusted advisor and public voice, leading the charge for AI regulation in New Zealand.

RAISE leadership spearheaded the open letter calling on the New Zealand Parliament to lead a bipartisan effort for risk management-based AI regulation and the establishment of a national AI oversight body. Appropriate regulation promotes, rather than inhibits, innovation by providing certainty on boundaries, spurring the development of solutions with confidence.
