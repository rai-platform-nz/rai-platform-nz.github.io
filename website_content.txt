            <div class="container">
                <h1>Economic Benefit</h1>
                <p class="hero-text" style="font-size: 1.2rem; max-width: 800px; margin-bottom: 2rem;">
                    AI could add $76b to New Zealand’s GDP by 2038, powering our future high-tech economy. However,
                    <a href="https://kpmg.com/nz/en/home/insights/2025/05/trust-attitudes-and-use-of-ai.html"
                        target="_blank">research</a> has shown that these productivity gains are undermined by
                    unreliable outputs, biased
                    decisions, and safety concerns (<a
                        href="https://www.ipsos.com/en-nz/understanding-aotearoa-new-zealand-ipsos-ai-monitor-2025"
                        target="_blank">Ipsos</a>).
                </p>

                <h2>Realizing Productivity Gains</h2>
                <p>
                    To realise these economic benefits, we need trustworthy AI, giving consumers and businesses the
                    confidence to engage.
                    Our vision is to establish NZ as a global leader in responsible AI (RAI) innovation and deployment,
                    where safety, integrity, and reliability drive our competitive edge.
                </p>

                <div class="grid">
                    <div class="card">
                        <h3>Sovereign AI Capability</h3>
                        <p>
                            We aim to build a sovereign large language model (LLM) with <a
                                href="https://catalystcloud.nz/" target="_blank">Catalyst Cloud</a>, <a
                                href="https://publicai.network/" target="_blank">Public AI Network</a>, and <a
                                href="https://metagov.org/" target="_blank">Metagov</a>, ensuring NZ retains control
                            over its critical AI infrastructure and data.
                        </p>
                    </div>

                    <div class="card">
                        <h3>Future-Ready Workforce</h3>
                        <p>
                            Through initiatives like the <a href="https://applieddoctorates.nz/" target="_blank">Applied
                                Doctorates</a> scheme and subsidised consulting for SMEs, we would upskill NZ's
                            workforce
                            and support the commercialisation of responsible AI.
                        </p>
                    </div>
                    <div class="card">
                        <h3>Export Advantage</h3>
                        <p>A focus on “trust by design” will be a key competitive advantage, enabling our exports to
                            thrive in international markets with complex regulatory environments.</p>
                    </div>
                </div>

                <h2 style="margin-top: 3rem;">Global Market</h2>
                <p>
                    The global market for responsible and explainable AI is expanding rapidly. Recent <a
                        href="https://www.lucintel.com/explainable-ai-market.aspx" target="_blank">estimates</a> project
                    the explainable AI market alone will exceed US$20 billion by 2030, reflecting demand in sectors such
                    as healthcare, finance, and government.
                </p>
            </div>

                <h1>Trustworthy AI</h1>
                <p style="font-size: 1.2rem; margin-bottom: 2rem;">
                    Appropriate use of AI offers significant economic and other benefits. However, AI innovation and
                    adoption are hampered by a lack of trust. Building trust demands more than technical sophistication.
                </p>

                <div class="grid">
                    <div class="card">
                        <h3>1. Trustworthy Technology</h3>
                        <p>Developing technical advancements in transparency, robustness, explainability, security, and
                            safety.</p>
                        <ul style="margin-top: 1rem; padding-left: 1.2rem;">
                            <li>Explainable and verifiable AI</li>
                            <li>Transparency in medical AI</li>
                            <li>Targeted GenAI models to mitigate hallucinations</li>
                        </ul>
                    </div>
                    <div class="card">
                        <h3>2. Trustworthy Frameworks</h3>
                        <p>Establishing thought leadership in accountability, ethics, human rights, governance, and
                            regulation.</p>
                        <ul style="margin-top: 1rem; padding-left: 1.2rem;">
                            <li>Shaping fit-for-purpose AI governance</li>
                            <li>Aligning with international best practices (<a
                                    href="https://www.oecd.org/en/topics/sub-issues/ai-principles.html"
                                    target="_blank">OECD</a>, <a
                                    href="https://www.unesco.org/en/artificial-intelligence/recommendation-ethics"
                                    target="_blank">UNESCO</a>)</li>
                            <li>Embedding ethical and legal norms</li>
                        </ul>
                    </div>
                    <div class="card">
                        <h3>3. Trustworthy Systems</h3>
                        <p>Building infrastructure and solutions collaboratively with industry and the public sector.
                        </p>
                        <ul style="margin-top: 1rem; padding-left: 1.2rem;">
                            <li>Sovereign Large Language Model (LLM)</li>
                            <li>Privacy-preserving environmental monitoring</li>
                            <li>Subsidised consulting for SMEs</li>
                        </ul>
                    </div>
