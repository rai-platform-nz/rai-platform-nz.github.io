Trustworthy AI

Appropriate use of AI offers significant economic and other benefits. However, AI innovation and adoption are hampered by a lack of trust. Building trust demands more than technical sophistication.

1. Trustworthy Technology

Developing technical advancements in transparency, robustness, explainability, security, and safety.

  • Explainable and verifiable AI
  • Transparency in medical AI
  • Targeted GenAI models to mitigate hallucinations

2. Trustworthy Frameworks

Establishing thought leadership in accountability, ethics, human rights, governance, and regulation.

  • Shaping fit-for-purpose AI governance
  • Aligning with international best practices (OECD, UNESCO)
  • Embedding ethical and legal norms

3. Trustworthy Systems

Building infrastructure and solutions collaboratively with industry and the public sector.

  • Sovereign Large Language Model (LLM)
  • Privacy-preserving environmental monitoring
  • Subsidised consulting for SMEs

Examples

Illustrative examples of potential projects include developing novel methods for explainable and verifiable AI to achieve transparency in medical AI diagnostic tools, and partnering Vision Mātauranga in an AI context.
